About poem

The poem starts with "It all began with a taboo",here the word taboo refers to menstruation,since menstruation is generally considered a taboo.
It is also periodical for women and it gives them pain and so the line goes as "A cycle of pain".
The next two lines talk about the purity of making love.The next two lines refer to a mother's womb.
The words "flower" and "butterfly" refer to a new born baby.
The following lines refer to 'pregnancy' and the word "product" refers to a new born baby.
The following lines refer to how fearful and wonderful we were and how happy we were being innocent.
