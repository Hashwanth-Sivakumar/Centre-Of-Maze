About poem

This poem talks about the gruesome crime,rape.
Here the body of a women is referred metaphorically to a 'Temple' and the word 'Blasphemed' refers to defiling of something sacred,which in this case the body of a 'woman'. (Biblical reference)
Similarly words like flower,pearl,fruit and ornament refer to 'women' and words like predator,swine,flirt,pervert,ghost and beast refer to 'rapists'.
The phrase 'Endless Slumber' refers to death.
